# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 23, 2026

## 1. Data Collection

Refinery is a **zero-retention** HTML processing engine. We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to third parties.

### What We Don't Do:
- **No Data Storage:** HTML content is processed in-memory and never persisted
- **No Logging:** We do not log your HTML payloads or processing results
- **No Third-Party APIs:** Refinery does not use external LLMs or inference services
- **No Data Transmission:** Your data never leaves the processing environment

## 2. Data Processing

### In-Memory Processing Only
- All HTML processing occurs in volatile memory
- Data is discarded immediately after processing
- No persistent storage of input or output data

### Deterministic Processing
- Refinery uses mathematical algorithms, not AI interpretation
- No machine learning models process your content
- Zero risk of data leakage to training datasets

## 3. Third-Party Services

### Apify Platform
- Refinery runs on the Apify platform infrastructure
- Apify's privacy policy applies to platform-level operations
- We do not share your data with Apify beyond necessary platform operations

### No External Integrations
- Refinery does not integrate with third-party analytics
- No tracking pixels or beacons are embedded in output
- No data is sent to external APIs for processing

## 4. Data Security

### Architecture
- **SOC2-Compliant Design:** Deterministic processing with audit-ready capabilities
- **Zero-LLM:** No inference engines that could leak data
- **No Steganographic Risk:** Mathematical processing eliminates instruction masking

### Enterprise Compliance
- **GDPR Ready:** No data retention means no cross-border transfer issues
- **Data Privacy Assured:** Zero third-party API calls
- **Audit Trail:** Processing logs available for compliance verification

## 5. Your Rights

### Data Ownership
- You retain full ownership of all data processed through Refinery
- Output data is yours to use, modify, or delete as you see fit
- No claim is made to processed content or results

### Data Deletion
- Since no data is stored, deletion is automatic upon processing completion
- No retention period means no data cleanup required
- Zero data footprint after processing

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

Refinery does not:
- Set cookies
- Use tracking scripts
- Collect browser fingerprints
- Monitor user behavior beyond necessary platform operations

## 7. Children's Privacy

Refinery is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Since no data is stored or transmitted to third parties, Refinery does not involve international data transfers. All processing occurs within the Apify platform infrastructure.

## 9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of material changes via the Apify platform.
